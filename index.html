The space provided by the platform is a virtual digital space that reconstructs the actual space. DimensionXYZ on the main page has been reconstructed with information on the space and walls of Hongdae with existing graffiti for the operation of the platform. The XYZ axis of the space is reconstructed by quantifying the size of the wall, the surrounding floating population, and the location information of the space, and the initial cost is set accordingly.

In the space, buildings where purchased digital graffiti can be installed are installed, and graphic animations that can indirectly recognize the public exist. In the future, the user-installed digital graffiti is exposed to the actual public through projection in the space before it is reorganized in the service.
